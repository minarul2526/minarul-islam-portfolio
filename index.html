About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Auburn University. My research focuses on reliable and context-aware machine learning for natural language, speech, security, and sensing applications.

I am interested in AI systems that can work reliably in real-world settings where inputs are noisy, ambiguous, shifted, or structurally complex.

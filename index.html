Welcome to my personal homepage

My name is Minarul Islam. I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Auburn University. My research focuses on reliable and context-aware machine learning for natural language, speech, security, and sensing applications.

My dissertation work focuses on Reliable and Context-Aware Sentiment Analysis under Linguistic Complexity. I study how machine learning models can make more reliable decisions when text contains distribution shift, discourse complexity, irony, aspect-level opinion, and domain variation.

Beyond my dissertation, I also work on speech model diagnostics, software and AI security, smartphone-based structural sensing, and deepfake audio detection. My broader goal is to build AI systems that are accurate, reliable, interpretable, and useful in real-world settings.
